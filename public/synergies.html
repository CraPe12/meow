Magic Chess: Go Go

Introductions:

Magic Chess: Go GO also known as Magic Chess is a strategy game that is played in a 7 by 6 chessboard battled by 8 players in a one versus all format led by your favorite MLBB heroes and other commanders to lead you in the battleground with your favorite MLBB heroes. Strategize, Create Synergy, Master, Dominate. This game is affiliated with Moonton available in both Android and IOS devices.

    Synergies

    Faction

    Moniyan Plains

    Moniyan Plains consisting of Guinevere, Silvanna, Lukas, Melissa, Lancelot and Odette is a faction synergy that gives its heroes extra attack speed and hybrid attack and will be multiplied every time they slay an enemy for six seconds.

    Northern Vale

    Northern Vale consisting of Masha, Atlas, Freya, Popol&Kupa, Aurora, and Yve is a faction synergy that deals true damage when hit by basic attacks six times by the Northern Vale heroes

    Dragon Altar

    Dragon Altar consisting of Yin, Akai, Wanwan, Sun, Ling, and Chang'e is a faction synergy that gives extra damage and defense for allies in the certain tiles in the battlefield that gives thrice the buff in dragon altar heroes and gains higher buffs when more Dragon Altar Heroes are in the battlefield and generates more Special tiles that will receive it's buff.

    Nature Spirit

    Nature Spirit consisting of Belerick, Bane, Grock, Joy, Kadita, and Estes is a faction synergy that combines and shares HP for all Nature Spirit heroes and gains extra Hybrid Defense when more Nature Spirit Heroes are in the battlefield.

    Astro Power

    Astro Power consisting of Hilda, Minotaur, Martis, Zhask, Karina and Lunox is a faction synergy that gives damage increase and hybrid lifesteal to the hero that is Astro Power that has the most ampunt of items and gains more damage increase when more Astro Power Heroes are in the battlefield.

    The Eruditio

    The Eruditio consisting of Lolita, Johnson, Layla, Nolan, Diggie and Natan is a faction synergy that gives an equipment from three equipments to choose from that scales depending your points if you win or lose the previous round, giving better equipemts if you came from a defeat abd when more The Eruditio heroes are placed the item will eventually have higher damage and if more are placed it will even heal your commander .

    The Inferno

    The Inferno consisting of Dyrroth Terizla, Thamuz, Moskov, Helcurt and Alice is a faction synergy that gives Inferno heroes basic attacks with Inferno Marks when dealing or taking damage which deals physical damage for 5 seconds stacking up to ten times which scales with how many unique Inferno heroes you've added.
